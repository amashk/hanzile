Hanzile: Guess the daily Chinese character

How to Play

Guess the hidden Chinese character in 6 attempts.

Making a guess

Type any Chinese character using your keyboard's IME and press Guess. Each guess reveals the strokes your character shares with the hidden one.

Reading the overlap %

After each guess you see a coloured tile showing the character and its pixel overlap with the target — how much of the stroke area matches.

木5% Low
本50% Medium
末89% High

The canvas

The large canvas shows a running reveal — every pixel your guesses share with the hidden character lights up. Use the colours below to understand each guess:

Your guess (人)
Hidden target (大)

Tip

Hover over a tile to see exactly where that character overlapped with the target.
